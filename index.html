A look into 2019/2020 Australian Bushfires

Utilising weather patterns to better prepare for future bushfires.

Satellite Imagery

NASA Satellite Data MODISC6 and VIIRS 375m from 2019-08-01 to 2020-01-11, a deep dive on fires captured from satellite imagery.

*Note Track is the no of pixels the perceived fire covered

Data provided by NASA.

Rainfall Patterns

A look at monthly rainfall patterns recorded during this period from various Australian weather stations. Rainfall may impact humidity, the abscence of may facilitate a environment prone to bushfires.

A look at monthly wind speed patterns recorded during this period from various Australian weather stations. High wind speeds can allow for fires to start and spread faster.
